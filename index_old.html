About

Design of the World is curated by Josephmark – a digital ventures practice that believes in the world-changing power of a great idea. It’s a space for us to shine a light on creativity we’re inspired by – design that’s educational, eco-friendly or just plain awesome. Design that’s independent, innovative or community-driven. And design that’s always – always – produced with ethics and integrity.

We love outstanding design and we scour the globe for the best examples. If you have an idea you think would be perfect for .W, please drop us an email – we’d love to hear from you.
