Soie de Lune

Luxurious hand-woven silks

Bespoke fabrics for the interior desing world

Soie de Lune (moon silk), was set up in 2002 and is registered in the United Kingdom & Lao PDR [Laos]. Soie de Lune was founded by Daniel Marcus and Anouphanethong Thammavong who formed this partnership not only to create luxurious hand-woven silks.
